Mian Qin is a PhD student in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania.

He is a member of the Patel Group. His broad interests include molecular simulation, scientific computing, and agentic research infrastructure.

He received a B.S. in Physics from Peking University in 2023. During his undergraduate studies, he was affiliated with the Xu Group.
